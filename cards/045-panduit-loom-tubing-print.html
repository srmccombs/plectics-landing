Installation Benefits
Slit Wall Design: The longitudinal slit allows cables to be easily inserted or removed without threading through the entire length. This feature significantly reduces installation time and allows for easy additions or modifications to existing cable bundles.

Corrugated Flexibility: The corrugated construction provides excellent flexibility for routing around corners and obstacles while maintaining protective strength.

UV Resistance & Outdoor Use
Specially formulated polyethylene provides excellent resistance to ultraviolet radiation, preventing degradation from sun exposure. Approved for outdoor installations where cables require protection from weather, moisture, and UV damage. Maintains flexibility and protective properties across wide temperature ranges.
Size Selection Guide
PANCLT50FC20 (0.51" ID): Suitable for small cable bundles, single large cables, or multiple small wires. Common for control wiring and small data cables.

PANCLT75FC20 (0.77" ID): Ideal for larger cable bundles, multiple data cables, or thicker power cables. Provides additional room for cable movement and heat dissipation.
