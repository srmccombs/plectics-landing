ESXF LED Floodlight Technical Specifications
Intended Use
The ESXF LED floodlight is designed for cost-effective, energy-efficient replacement of traditional sources ranging from 150W quartz up to 1000W metal halide. Ideal for parking lots, signage, yards, walkways, landscaping, warehouses, and security applications. Features uniform wide flood 7x7 light distribution.

Installation Options
ESXF1 & ESXF2: Ship with ½" NPS threaded knuckle mount factory installed. Mount to conduit bodies or 4" electrical boxes using included round mounting plate. Yoke mounts easily changed in field for solid surface mounting. (ESXF1 P0 yoke purchased separately)

ESXF3 & ESXF4: Include yoke mounting for solid surfaces and integral slipfitter that mates with standard 2⅜" tenons for pole-top mounting. All models ship with 18" SO cord.
