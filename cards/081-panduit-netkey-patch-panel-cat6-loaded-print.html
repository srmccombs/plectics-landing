NetKey® Cat 6 Pre-Populated Punchdown Patch Panel

Innovative Cat 6 flat punchdown patch panel delivers valuable performance, organization with clear labeling, and universal compatibility - ideal for network installations and upgrades. Pre-populated with jacks - Ready to terminate, no need to purchase separate modules. Category 6 performance - Meets all TIA/EIA-568-C.2 Category 6 and ISO 11801 Class E requirements at frequencies up to 250 MHz. T568A/T568B wiring - Supports both standard wiring schemes. PoE support - Supports IEEE 802.3af/802.3at and proposed 802.3bt type 3 and type 4 PoE applications. Flat style design - Organized port layout with integrated label areas. 19-inch rack mount - Standard equipment rack compatible. Cold rolled steel construction - Durable and sturdy. UL 1863 approved. RoHS Compliant - Environmentally responsible.
