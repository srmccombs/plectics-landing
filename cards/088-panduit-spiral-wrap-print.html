Panduit Spiral Wrap Specifications
Product Overview
Panduit Spiral Wrap provides superior abrasion protection while allowing for flexible cable management. The spiral design enables easy bundling of multiple cables into a single, organized run while maintaining the ability to branch out individual cables at any point along the length. Each package includes an installation tool for easy application.
Material Options
Polyethylene
General purpose material offering good flexibility, abrasion resistance, and chemical resistance. Natural or black color options available. Suitable for most indoor and outdoor applications.
Nylon 6.6
Enhanced mechanical properties with superior strength and temperature resistance. Excellent abrasion resistance and durability in demanding environments. Natural color.
Flame Retardant Polyethylene
Special formulation meeting flame retardant requirements for safety-critical applications. Black color. Maintains flexibility while providing enhanced fire safety characteristics.

Benefits of Spiral Design
The spiral wrap design provides unique advantages over solid sleeving or other bundling methods. Individual cables can be easily routed in or out at any point without unwrapping the entire bundle. This allows for flexible installation and easy modifications. The overlapping spiral layers provide excellent abrasion protection while maintaining flexibility for routing around corners and through tight spaces.
