Arlington TV Box Specifications
Product Overview
Arlington's recessed steel TV Box is the best way to mount flat screen TVs flush against a wall. Designed for use in commercial construction where metal raceway is used, Steel TV Box provides power and/or low voltage in one or more of the openings. Plugs and connectors stay inside the box, without extending past the wall, allowing furniture and TVs to sit flush against the wall surface.
Construction & Design
Steel Box Construction
Durable steel box with non-metallic paintable trim plate available in white or black. Built-in voltage separator keeps power and low voltage connections properly isolated for code compliance. Optional blank covers available for unused boxes.
Installation Methods
New Construction
Box screw-mounts directly to wood or steel studs using #6 screws. Install box, pull wires, install voltage separator, then add drywall. After drywall installation, add trim plate and install devices.
Retrofit/Old Work
Cut hole using enclosure as template. Mounting wings on 2-gang and 3-gang styles hold box securely against wall. Insert box in hole, tighten wing screws, then install trim plate and devices.

Advantages Over Standard Boxes
Unlike standard electrical boxes that allow plugs and connectors to extend past the wall surface, Arlington's recessed TV Box keeps everything flush. This allows TVs, furniture, and appliances to sit directly against the wall without interference from protruding plugs. The result is a cleaner, more professional appearance and better space utilization.
