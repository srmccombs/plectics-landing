Erico Caddy Mounting Bracket Specifications
Product Overview
Low voltage mounting brackets for Class 2 wiring applications. Eliminates need for electrical boxes while providing positive support with locking screws. Available in steel and plastic options for various installation requirements.
Model Specifications
Steel Models (ERIMPLS/ERIMPLS2/ERIMP1S)
Pregalvanized steel construction with locking screws. 1-Gang and 2-Gang configurations. ERIMP1S designed for new construction stud mounting. Durable metal for commercial installations. 100 pieces per package.
Plastic Model (ERIMP1P)
ABS construction with serrated interlocking system. No sharp edges, no installation tools required. Template points for easy marking. Recessed design for flush device mounting. 100 pieces per package.

Installation Steps
Steel: Cut opening, fish wire, insert bracket, tighten screws, install device.
Plastic: Mark with template, cut opening, fish wire, push bracket (auto-locks), install device.
