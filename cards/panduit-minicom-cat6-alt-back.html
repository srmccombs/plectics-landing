Panduit Mini-Com TX6 PLUS - Cat 6 Jack Module (CJ688TG**)
Fast selection + fewer trips back to the truck

Best use cases

- Workstation drops, patch panels, and telecom rooms
- New installs or quick swaps; supports T568A or T568B
- High-performance copper runs (Category 6 / Class E, 250 MHz)

Install in about 60 seconds (TG style)

1) Strip jacket; keep pairs twisted as close as possible.
2) Place conductors into the cap following the built-in T568A/B label.
3) Close cap (forward motion) - no punchdown tool required.
4) Snap jack into Mini-Com faceplate, patch panel, or surface box.

Common add-ons (boosts first-trip completion)

- Faceplates (1/2/4 port) - Surface mount boxes - Patch panels
- Patch cords (match color for ID) - Velcro / cable management
- Labels/icons - Dust caps - Port blockers (security)

Specs at a glance

  • Category: Cat 6 / Class E (1-250 MHz)
  • Cable range: 22-26 AWG, 4-pair UTP (100 ohm)
  • Termination: TG style, forward motion, maintains pair geometry
  • Wiring: Universal label for T568A & T568B
  • Power: Supports PoE, PoE+ and PoE++ (up to 100W)
  • Compliance: RoHS; UL listings vary by configuration
