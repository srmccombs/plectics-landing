Rack Bonding Busbar (CHT10610019)
19" rack-mount copper bar for equipment bonding. 3/16" x 3/4" hard-drawn 110 alloy copper. Eight #6-32 tapped holes. Includes hardware kit with splice plate, screws, washers, ring terminals.
Secondary Bonding 12" (CHT13622012)
2"H x 12"W wall-mount busbar. 300 series stainless steel. ANSI/TIA-607 & BICSI compliant. 3/8" holes spaced 5.75" apart. Wall/floor/ceiling mount. UL File E236184.
Primary Bonding 12" (CHT40153012)
4"H x 12"W wall-mount busbar for bonding backbones. Stainless steel construction. Central attachment point for ICT systems. TIA-607-E compliant. Includes insulators.
